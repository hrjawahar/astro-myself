D1 — Rashi Chart (Birth Chart)
This is your primary birth chart. It shows the actual positions of all 9 planets in the sky at the moment you were born, divided across 12 houses. Think of it as a map of the life you were born into — the promises, challenges, and natural tendencies written into your existence from the start. D1 is always read first.
D9 — Navamsha Chart (Soul & Maturity Chart)
The D9 is a divisional chart derived mathematically from your D1. Each sign in D1 is divided into 9 equal parts — your D9 shows which of those parts each planet falls in. It reveals the deeper, soul-level quality of each planet's expression, and most importantly shows how the D1 promises actually mature and deliver in the second half of life. If D1 promises something and D9 confirms it, the result is strong and lasting. If D9 contradicts D1, the early promise fades.

What is Vimshottari Dasha?

Dasha is the timing system of Vedic astrology — it divides your 120-year life span into planetary periods, each ruled by a different planet. During each Dasha, that planet's themes and energy dominate your life experience. Understanding which Dasha you are currently in, and how that planet sits in your specific chart, is the key to precise life predictions.

Each Maha Dasa (major period) is subdivided into Antar Dasas (sub-periods). The current Antar Dasa shows where the focus is within the broader Maha Dasa theme.
